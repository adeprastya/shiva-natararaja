About Shiva Natararaja

Nataraja portrays Lord Shiva as the ultimate creator and destroyer, engaged in the process of the life cycle, creation, preservation, and destruction of the universe.

Symbolism of Shiva Natararaja

Tandava Dance

Represents cosmic cycles - life's rhythm, birth, death, and change.

Four Arms

Carries symbolic items - drum (creation's sound), fire (destructive force), assurance/protection, and grace/control.

Ring of Fire

Symbolizes cosmic energy enclosing the universe, signifying time's cyclical nature and transformation.

Raised Leg

Signifies liberation from ignorance, stepping into enlightenment.

Hair & Crescent Moon

Wild hair symbolizes divine energy, moon signifies time's cycles.

Cosmic Balance

Standing on ignorance, symbolizes knowledge's triumph.

Dual Nature

Harmonious coexistence of creation, destruction, life, death - cyclical existence.
